Add PanicSafe to Home Screen

Open panicsafe-safety.vercel.app in Safari, then follow these steps.

  1. Tap the iOS Share button in Safari.
  2. Choose Add to Home Screen.
  3. Tap Add. PanicSafe will be added to your HomeScreen.
